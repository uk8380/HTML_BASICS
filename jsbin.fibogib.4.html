HOW TO PREPARE CHICKEN FIRED RICE

BACHELOR HACK'S
  1. CHICKEN FIRED RICE:
    • To make Chicken Fried Rice, you will need very few ingredients
    • Chicken – I used chicken breast and boiled them until they were cooked properly. Then I shred them using a fork. You can also use a hand mixer to shred the cooked chicken breast.
    • Cornflour – Coating the shredded chicken in some cornflour and then frying it gives it a nice and crispy texture. I also added a little bit of soy sauce to the chicken before frying.
    • Sesame Oil – I fried the rice in sesame oil which gives it a very asian flavour. You can however choose any oil that you like.
    • Egg – If you have ever ordered Chicken Fried Rice from any Asian take away, You must have noticed that it always comes with scrambled eggs.
    • Garlic – I love garlic in my Asian recipes and I add a lot of it.
    • Spring Onion and Veggies – These add a nice crunch to the rice. You can chose to add your fav veggies in this rice. Mushroom, broccoli, sweet peas, zucchini etc go well.
    • Sauces – Asian sauces like Soy sauce, Vinegar Red Chilli sauce etc go in this recipe. Feel free to adjust the quantity of these according to your taste.
    • Rice – Use short grain rice for a typical Asian style chicken fried rice.
    • Also make sure the rice is cooked well in advance and cooled properly before stir frying. A day old rice is the best.
